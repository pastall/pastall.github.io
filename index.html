Our Love Story

So, what led us here?
Well, we’ve known each other now for the best part of 8.5 years and have been together for 5.5 of them...

Jan and I originally met in a night club in Manchester’s Northern Quarter called North where I got introduced to her by a friend he was out with. I was pretty clear that my name was indeed Phil (not surprising) when I introduced myself yet Jan insisted to call me Paul for the duration of the night, and to top things off, as Jan was in her well known ‘torment mode’, I ended up with a bucket of ice all over me as she was chasing someone around the room having a water fight… Needless to say, Jan made a great impression on me that evening.

Our paths would always cross as we were/are part of the same circle of friends from then on in and Jan eventually got to grips calling me Phil! Phew! Various situations ensued and eventually we would end up in a hot tub one sunny Saturday afternoon and let’s just say the rest is history…

The Plot

Getting the ring on Jan’s finger
I had plotted for nearly 6 months plus to achieve the most amazing location, ring and environment to ask that big question. The chosen venue was Reykjavik, Iceland whilst we attempted to achieve a life long ambition to see the Northern Lights.

Friday: The first evening was a flop, totally clouded over so no chance of seeing them.

Saturday: Eating our evening meal we decided no matter what that we’d be stood outside in an attempt to find them. You are told to take pictures even if you can’t see them in the skies as a camera can show them much sooner than the eyes can pick them up. I became the happy snapper and as luck would have it one of the pictures showed the start of a light show. We spent the next hour watching the show peak and slow down and at that point we decided to head back in… At that point, I asked Jan to come outside on our balcony overlooking the fjord, which she did (reluctantly) – It was here that I asked her to marry me. As luck would have it, the lights were still showing as well so someone really was looking over me that night!

Sunday (Our Last Evening): We saw THE MOST AMAZING light show...

Not too bad a result, 2 nights from 3 seeing the Northern Lights and getting engaged as well! PERFECT...

The Venue

OF THE MARRIAGE
We’ve chosen to get married abroad in the most spectacular of venues. We’re not really people that are fussed with tradition so we wanted to go somewhere that was truly unique and we’re sure you’ll agree that we’ve achieved that!
DATE & LOCATION

The location is in Dubrovnik, Croatia and is in the most amazing cave under a hotel, the Hotel More. We've picked up the hotels nearby the venue so you can find the right one for you!

THE HOTEL

The hotel exposed this cave, we were told, when they were digging the footings for the hotel itself and they new immediately that they had to make a feature out of it and we’re really pleased they did.

THE CAVE

The cave itself will be where we will have the ceremony and we will have the reception outside right next to the sea overlooking the bay and watching the sun set. It’s a beautiful place to be!
